Connect Clique

A social planner where friends suggest activities, vote on dates, and keep track of plans in an interactive calendar.

As a user, you can add your friends, create a new event, vote for your friends' activities e finally see your planned activities on an interactive calendar.

Pitched this idea to my peers and It was selected as a final bootcamp project.

Designed the prototype in Figma, and implemented the DB using Active Record on Rails. Developed models and CRUD functionality, integrated user authentications (Devise), and gems Authorization and Pundit, configured image hosting and uploads, and deployed the application on Heroku.
Collaborated in a group of 4 via Github, using branching, pull requests, code reviews.
