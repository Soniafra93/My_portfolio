Connect Clique

A social planner where friends suggest activities, vote on dates, and keep track of plans in an interactive calendar.

As a user, you can add your friends, create a new event, vote for your friends' activities e finally see your planned activities on an interactive calendar.

Programming & Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Node.js), Ruby on Rails.
Databases & Deployment: PostgreSQL, Active Record, Heroku.
Design & Tools: Figma, Cloudinary, Notion, Slack, Trello.
Version Control: Git, GitHub.

I pitched this idea to my bootcamp colleagues and It was chosen to be developed as a final project.
The goal of the project was to build a full stack application in a group effort.
I was responsible for the design of the prototype on Figma, the DB with Active Record on Rails, Models and CRUD, gems Authorization and Pundit, Devise, Hosting and Image Upload.
